11

Design Principles

Trust First

Near-black #121213 primary, clean white space, no gimmicks. Every decision reinforces credibility.

Approachable Authority

Blue CTAs signal action and modernity; near-black headings signal confidence and depth.

Clarity Over Decoration

System fonts, predictable spacing, minimal shadow use. If it doesn't serve the message, remove it.

Action-Oriented

#106EEA Dodger Blue for all primary CTAs — always unambiguous, always visible.

Warm Professionalism

Blue tints (#F1F6FE, #E2EEFD) soften the dark palette without feeling clinical or cold.
